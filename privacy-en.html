ReadLens is a fully local application. All your data is stored on your device. We never collect, store, or transmit your personal data.

1. What Data Do We Collect?

Short Answer: Nothing

ReadLens does not collect any personal data. All data is stored locally on your device.

Detailed Explanation

ReadLens stores the following data only on your device:

  • Saved Articles: Web content you choose to save
  • AI Summaries: Summaries generated by your API Key (if enabled)
  • Tags and Notes: Tags and personal notes you add to articles
  • Settings: Your app preferences (theme, language, etc.)
  • API Keys: Your AI service API Keys (encrypted locally)

All of the above data is stored only on your device. The ReadLens team cannot access it.

  • ‚úÖ No Third-Party Tracking: No Google Analytics or any tracking tools

3. AI Features & API Keys

üîë BYOK (Bring Your Own Key) Model

ReadLens uses a "bring your own key" approach. This means:

Your API Keys

  • Encrypted Storage: API Keys are stored with AES-256 encryption on your device
  • Direct Connection: ReadLens sends your content directly to AI providers (OpenAI, Claude, etc.)
  • No Middleman: We have no intermediate servers; we cannot see or log your requests
  • Your Control: You can delete or change API Keys anytime

Data Transfer to AI Providers

When you use AI summary features:

  • Your article content is sent directly from your browser to your chosen AI provider
  • This data does not go through ReadLens servers (we don't have any)
  • Please review each AI provider's privacy policy:
    • OpenAI Privacy Policy
    • Anthropic (Claude) Privacy Policy
    • Google (Gemini) Privacy Policy

4. Chrome Extension Permissions

ReadLens requires the following Chrome permissions:

activeTab (Current Tab)

Purpose: Read content from the webpage you're viewing to save articles

Data Destination: Stored only on your local device

storage & unlimitedStorage

Purpose: Store articles, settings, and API Keys on your device

Data Destination: Stored only in your local Chrome storage

contextMenus (Context Menu)

Purpose: Provide right-click quick save functionality

Data Destination: No data collection

notifications

Purpose: Show save success/failure notifications

Data Destination: No data collection

https://*/*(Network Access)

Purpose: Extract content from webpages and connect to AI provider APIs

Data Destination: Only sent to your chosen AI provider (if AI features enabled)

6. Data Security

Local Encryption

  • API Keys are stored with AES-256 encryption
  • Encryption keys are stored in your browser
  • Even if someone accesses your device storage, they cannot read API Keys without decryption keys
